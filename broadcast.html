Start Broadcast
It's extremely simple to start broadcasting and make money on GameCaster. It all runs in your browser! Just make sure to allow the necessary plugins for your browser (microphone, webcam(optional), etc).

When your ready, click 'Start Broadcast'. It will allow you to select a video input source. Your computer screen or your webcam. Then, it will ask for an audio input source. Select the mic you use, and then the broadcast will begin!

Once the broadcast has been started, you'll be redirected to a control panel for your broadcast. It will add audio level control, facecam size, and which source to stream from.

Your custom streaming site will automatically add a tipping and subscription system where viewers can tip money or subscribe for $5.00 per month. There are also automatic stream alerts in a custom overlay by us that you pick on the 'Edit Site' page.
